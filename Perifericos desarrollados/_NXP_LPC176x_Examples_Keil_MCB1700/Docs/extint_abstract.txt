The EINT project is a simple program for the NXP 'LPC1768' 
microcontroller.
When press EINT0 sequentially, P2.0~3 and P2.4~7 will output
high logic alternately.


Example functionality:                                                   
 - Clock Settings:
   - XTAL  =  12 MHz
   - PLL0  = 288 MHz
   - CCLK  =  72 MHz
